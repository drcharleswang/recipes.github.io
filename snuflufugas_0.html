1. How to make Mac & Cheese

What you need:
3 Cups of macaroni
2/3 Cup of cheese
1 Tbsp of salt

Serves 6

Fill a medium-size pot half-way with water. Dump the macaroni in the pot. set the stove to "power boil". Dump salt. Boil for 8 min. Now, drain the water. Set the stove to 5. Dump the cheese. Stir 3-5 min. Enjoy!
