2. How to make Cinnamon Chips & Fruit Salsa

You will need:
2 Big tortillas
Cinnamon
Brown sugar
6/8 Cup of diced strawberries
4/8 Cup of diced apples
2 Tbsp of any fruit juice

Serves 3

Sprinkle cinnamon and brown sugar on the tortillas. Cut them into 8 slices. put them in the oven at 375 for 10 min. Now, start making the salsa. Put the strawberries, apples, and juice in a bowl and mix it. Take out the chips and enjoy!
